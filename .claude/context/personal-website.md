## About Me

I'm a **data scientist**, though my day-to-day is a bit messier than that label suggests. I work at **Revcloud**, a lead gen AI startup, on a product called **Alyson AI**, specifically the **Experiments Manager**. Some days I'm building **frontend and backend** or working through **system design**. Other days I'm deep in **ML pipelines and model development**. There's also the part where I coordinate a few developers and ML engineers. Startups don't really do tidy job descriptions, and I kind of love that. You do what the product needs.

On the side, I volunteer with **[Forengers](https://www.forengers.in/)**, a Pune-based NGO working on climate change, where I **head the Tech Team**. That means website upkeep, a growing pile of internal automations (we recently shipped a **digital certification system**, which I'm pretty happy about), and anything else that needs someone technical to dig in. I also help with central coordination for the **Operations Team**.

Back in undergrad, I was part of the **mechanical design team** for our college's [BAJA SAE](https://www.instagram.com/teamkraftwagen) entry, working with **SolidWorks**, **Ansys**, free body diagrams, and simulation runs that took forever. We **built an ATV from scratch** for a **national competition**. Exhausting, slightly unhinged, and still the best hands-on learning experience I've had.

When I'm not at a computer, I'm usually playing **badminton** or making mildly embarrassing attempts at wooden staff spinning and (pretend) sword-fighting (don't ask). I'm also a bit of a **compulsive note taker**, which is how the pile of notes [above](https://www.adityajoshi.in/#resources) came to exist. If you're a DS/ML person, maybe they'll help. If not, hopefully they're at least mildly entertaining.
